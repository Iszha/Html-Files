"HealthTrack" is a mobile application developed to assist individuals in managing chronic health conditions. The app allows users to track vital health metrics such as blood pressure, blood sugar levels, and heart rate. It provides personalized health recommendations and reminders for medication intake and doctor's appointments. Health data is securely stored and can be shared with healthcare providers for remote monitoring. The app aims to improve the quality of life for individuals living with chronic diseases.
